6+ Years Of Experience

Established in 2014, Studio AX3 is a high tech company specialized in: Building construction and Civil works, Building finishes, Aluminium Shopfront and Glass panel installations, Furniture and Wood work, Dry wall and Office partitioning.

With an excellent workforce, Studio AX3 has contributed immensely to Nigeria's economic and industrial growth since incorporation. Backed with modern, cutting-edge technology, the company has over the years made a good name for itself.

Motivated by a vision of excellence, we invest in both qualitative material and skilled manpower to ensure customers satisfaction.

Our Mission

To create a positive and lasting impact in the Construction Industry by providing the best quality in building and building finishes. We are committed to ensuring that standards are met and projects are delivered in timely fashion without compromising on Customer satisfaction, quality and integrity.

Our Vision

To achieve the best possible quality and standard of the construction market while maintaining our niche for integrity, profitability and establishing our companyas the preferred construction company in Nigeria.
